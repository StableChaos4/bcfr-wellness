About The Program

Dedicated to helping our firefighters “Stay Healthy, Stay Fit, and Stay Committed.”

Our Leadership

The BCFR Wellness Committee is comprised of dedicated members focused on the physical and mental longevity of our personnel. We strive to provide evidence-based resources and peer-driven support.

Our Mission

To foster a culture of health that recognizes the unique stresses of the fire service and provides the tools necessary to manage them effectively.
