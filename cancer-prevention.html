The "Clean Cab" Philosophy

Brevard County Fire Rescue is committed to reducing carcinogen exposure. Our goal is to ensure that the pollutants we face on the fireground stay on the fireground.

Post-fire decontamination is not an option; it is a requirement for the long-term health of our crews and their families.

Post-Exposure Steps

  • 1. Gross Decon: Rinse gear on-scene prior to leaving the box.
  • 2. Wipes: Use specialized wipes for neck, face, and hands immediately.
  • 3. Clean Cab: No contaminated gear or SCBAs in the passenger compartment.
  • 4. Shower within the Hour: Prioritize a hot shower as soon as you return to quarters.
